## Design Specifications for All Templates

### Typography
- **Headings**: Clean, sans-serif (Inter, Roboto, or Poppins) - 700-900 weight
- **Body**: Readable sans-serif - 400-500 weight
- **Line Height**: 1.6 for body, 1.3 for headings
- **Letter Spacing**: Minimal for professional feel

### Spacing System
- Base unit: 4px
- Spacing scale: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64px
- Content padding: 20-24px on mobile, 32px on desktop
- Component gaps: 16-24px between major sections

### Component Styles

#### Cards
- Border radius: 8-12px (healthcare feel)
- Subtle shadows only (no harsh shadows)
- Clean borders: 1px subtle lines
- Hover: Slight lift effect (2-4px), enhanced shadow
- Padding: 16-24px internal

#### Buttons
- Primary: Full opacity, solid color
- Secondary: Ghost style with border
- Status: Color-coded (green for done, orange for upcoming)
- Border radius: 8px (slightly rounded, professional)
- Padding: 12-16px × 24-32px

#### Progress & Status
- Circular progress for overall completion
- Horizontal bars for individual vaccine progress
- Color coding: Green (completed), Orange (upcoming), Gray (future)
- Clean, minimalist indicators

#### Vaccine Cards
- Vertical card layout
- Status indicator on top-right
- Icon on left, details on right
- Clear date and description
- Call-to-action button or status badge

### Animation
- Smooth transitions: 300ms cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
- Entrance animations: Gentle fade-in and slide-up
- Hover effects: Subtle, professional (not playful)
- No excessive animations (healthcare context)

### Icons & Imagery
- Clean, minimal icons (Feather or Heroicons style)
- Healthcare-focused imagery
- Illustrations: Professional, not cartoonish
- Color consistency across all icons
